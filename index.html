About

Hi, I’m Ali! I’ve been baking since before I can remember, sitting on the kitchen counter as a small child and watching my mother and grandmother create magic in the oven. I sold. My first custom desserts in high school baking in my parent’s kitchen after I finished my homework. Now, as a full-time graduate student Cloud 9 Cookies is my artistic outlet and the alter ego to my academic work. I absolutely love the process of creating a fully custom set of cookies to celebrate the big and little things. As someone with a sweet tooth that rivals a kid in a candy store, I can attest that my cookies taste as good as they look. Drop me a line to begin an order--I can’t wait to create edible art for you!

FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How far in advance should I place my order?

Orders must be placed a minimum of 3 weeks before your pick-up date. This allows me time to plan and create the perfect set of custom cookies for your event. Good things take time!

Do you ever take last minute orders?

Last minute orders will be assessed on a case-by-case basis depending on availability. Orders placed withing 7 days of the pick-up date will incur a $25 per dozen rush fee.

Do you ever hold holiday pre-sales or flash sales?

Yes! Keep an eye out on my Instagram @cloud.9.cookies for holiday pre-sales and flash sales. Pre-sales allow you to order semi-customizable sets of cookies a few days in advance. Flash sales are sets of ready-to-go cookies which are sold on a first come first served basis.

How much do your cookies cost?

Cookies cost $72 per dozen before tax. This includes individually sealing each cookie to maintain freshness and basic packaging for sets, like a bakery box. Please note custom cookies have a 2-dozen minimum order.

I know I want cookies for an event, but I don’t know what I want them to look like. Is that ok?

That is more than ok! I truly see my cookies as art you can eat, and love to the process of designing a set. When you submit an order request, please also share a bit about your event, and invitation if you have one, a pinterest board, color scheme, or other broad event design information. I’ll take it from there.

I saw an amazing set on social media, and I want you to recreate it but with my details or colors. Will you do this?

I’m sorry, but no. I am more than happy to draw inspiration from other cookies, but I do not think it is right to copy another artist’s work. The price you pay for my cookies includes fully custom design work because I want each cookie set to be as unique as my wonderful clients.

How long do your cookies stay fresh?

All cookies come individually sealed and will stay fresh at room temperature for 4 weeks as long as they are kept in their bags or in an airtight container.

Do you offer delivery?

I may be able to offer delivery depending on availability for an additional fee, so let me know if this is something you are interested in when you submit your order request.

I have an allergy. Can you accommodate me?

Unfortunately, I cannot. All cookies are produced in a home kitchen that is not subject to state licensure or inspection and that may also process common food allergens such as tree nuts, peanuts, eggs, soy, wheat, milk, fish and crustacean shellfish.

Do you ship?

Unfortunately, not at the moment. All orders must be picked up in Loveland, Colorado.

Do you do sweets other than frosted sugar cookies?

Nope, but I have some super talented friends who do. Let me know if you’d like a referral.

Do you offer vegan, dairy, or gluten free options?

Unfortunately, not at the moment.
