About

Hi, I’m Ali! I’ve been baking since before I can remember, sitting on the kitchen counter as a small child and watching my mother and grandmother create magic in the oven. I sold. My first custom desserts in high school baking in my parent’s kitchen after I finished my homework. Now, as a full-time graduate student Cloud 9 Cookies is my artistic outlet and the alter ego to my academic work. I absolutely love the process of creating a fully custom set of cookies to celebrate the big and little things. As someone with a sweet tooth that rivals a kid in a candy store, I can attest that my cookies taste as good as they look. Drop me a line to begin an order--I can’t wait to create edible art for you!
